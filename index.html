I'm a UX/UI designer with a graphic design background. Graduated from DESIGNATION, Chicago. Who likes people and loves designing smart solutions for their problems.

Three experiences have shaped my career. Working in my family’s business, I focused on developing strong interpersonal skills. Majoring in art history in college taught me to explore and articulate the power of design. As an intelligence analyst for the US Army, I learned how to synthesize data and create strategies.

Outside of designing for experience, I enjoy dog photography, swimming, hiking and running in the rain. If you want to brainstorm design solutions or share your interests with me, let’s get in touch.
