The Graide Network

Connecting teachers with students majoring in education to assist grading student work.

THE BRIEF

The Graide Network connects teachers with undergraduate education majors who can help them grade student work. Launched September 2015, the actual grading currently takes place off-platform via downloadable Excel templates. Our task was to prepare low- to mid-fidelity interaction design recommendations for how the teacher’s side of this process will work on the platform. We began by doing user interviews and usability testing of the current process (as well as broader field research).
