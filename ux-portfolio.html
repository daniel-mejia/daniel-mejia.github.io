about me

My name is daniel mejia,
pleased to meet you!

I'm  constantly curious about the source of problems and how to solve them. As a former graphic designer and current UX/UI designer graduated from DESIGNATION, I now get to learn about the user and design smart solutions for their problems.

Three experiences have shaped my career. Working in my family’s business, I focused on developing strong interpersonal skills. Majoring in art history in college taught me to explore and articulate the power of design. As an intelligence analyst for the US Army, I learned how to synthesize data and create strategies.

Outside of designing for experience, I enjoy swimming, running in the rain, dog photography and hiking. If you want to brainstorm design solutions or chat about these interests let’s get in touch.
